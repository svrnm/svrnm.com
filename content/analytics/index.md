This website uses [Umami](https://umami.is/) to collect website analytics,
if you provide consent.

### What is collected

When you consent to analytics, the following information is collected:

- **Page views**: Which pages you visit
- **Referrer**: Where you came from (e.g., a search engine or another website)
- **Browser & OS**: General information about your browser and operating system
- **Country**: Your approximate location based on your IP address (the IP address itself is not stored)
- **Device type**: Whether you're using a desktop, tablet, or mobile device


### Your choice

You can change your analytics preference at any time using the small icon in the bottom-right corner of every page. Your choice is stored locally in your browser and is respected across all pages.

- **Yes**: Analytics will be enabled, and anonymous usage data will be collected
- **No**: No analytics script will be loaded, and no data will be collected

If you don't make a choice, no data is collected and the website works perfectly fine without analytics.

### Why analytics?

Analytics help me understand which content is useful and how visitors find this website. This helps me improve the content and prioritize what to write about.
